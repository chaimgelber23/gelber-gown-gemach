## Appointments

- Each slot is **15 minutes**. Visits often last up to 30 minutes, so please do not overstay your time to allow others their turn.
- **Maximum 4 people** per group.
- Groups of **6 people** should book a 30-minute appointment or take the last slot of the evening.

---

## How to Book

**Text 718-614-8390** with the following information:

1. Name
2. Date and time you'd like to come
3. Number of people in your group
4. Wedding date
5. Phone number

---

## Gown Sizes

We carry girls' and women's gowns from little girls up to 1X.

---

## Pickup / Return

- **Pickup:** Gown(s) can be picked up two weeks before your wedding (text to arrange pickup time).
- **Return:** Please return the gowns on or before the Motzaei Shabbos after your wedding with your donation.
- The door to the Gemach is open; you can return the gown at any time.

---

## Alterations

You may alter the gown, but:
- You **cannot cut the gown at all**.
- Seamstress should use larger stitches so the work can be taken out easily.
