# Gelber Gown Gemach — Frequently Asked Questions

## Location & Access

**Q: Where is the entrance?**
A: In the garage at the end of the driveway, on the left side of the house (1327 East 26th Street, Brooklyn, NY 11210).

---

## Appointments

**Q: What are the appointment hours?**
A: Wednesday 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM and Motzei Shabbos 7:30 PM – 9:30 PM (by appointment only).

**Q: How do I book?**
A: Text 718-614-8390 with these 5 items:
1. Your name
2. Date and time you'd like to come
3. Number of people in your group
4. Wedding date
5. Phone number

**Q: How many people can come?**
A: Maximum 4 people per group. Groups of 6 need to book a 30-minute appointment or take the last slot of the evening.

---

## Gowns

**Q: What sizes do you have?**
A: We carry girls' and women's gowns from little girls up to 1X.

**Q: When can I pick up?**
A: Two weeks before your wedding. Text to arrange a pickup time.

**Q: When do I return?**
A: On or before the Motzaei Shabbos after your wedding, along with your donation. The door is open; you can return at any time.

**Q: Can I alter it?**
A: Yes, but no cutting allowed. Ask your seamstress to use larger stitches so the work can be taken out easily.

---

## Donation

**Q: How much is the donation?**
A: $100 standard. Chinuch/Kollel families donate at their discretion. Checks payable to "Gelber" or cash.
